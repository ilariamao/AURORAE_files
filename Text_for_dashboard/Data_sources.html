Data sources

European Environmental Agency (EEA)

The EEA website provides access to air quality measurements time series.

Reference: EEA, EEA (2024).Air Quality Download Service (Accessed June 27, 2024).

Finnish Meteorological Institute (FMI)

The FMI Download Observations service website provides access to air quality measurements time series on its network in Finland including PM10.

Reference: FMI, FMI (2024).Open Data Download Service (Accessed June 27, 2024).

Copernicus Atmospheric Monitoring Service (CAMS)

The Atmosphere Data Store (ADS) service is the main point of access CAMS data.

Reference:

PM10 forecast: Generated using CAMS European air quality forecast (Accessed June 27, 2024)

Meteorological varibales (Temperature at 2 m, U and V components of the wind, Boundary Layer Height, Mean Sea Level Pressure, Total Precipitation): Those data are generated by the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF) and distributed by
CAMS global atmospheric composition forecasts (Accessed June 27, 2024).

How to protect your health

Here are some effective tips to protect your health in case of high air pollution level (ORANGE and RED colors on the AURORAE map, PM10 concentration higher than 45 µg/m³>)

  • Wear close-fitting particulate respirators such as N-95 or FFP2 and FFP3 facemasks when going outdoor
  • Avoid exercising outdoors and especcially close to high-traffic areas or close to air pollution sources
  • Shift from motorised to active travel such as cycling or walking wearing a facemask
  • Keep windows and doors closed to avoid deterioration of indoor air quality
  • Avoid heating your home and cooking using the fireplace and woodstoves
  • Check daily the AQURORAE air quality forecast and maps to reduce you exposure to air pollution
